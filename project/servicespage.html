Step-by-Step Dental Care Process

Initial Check-up

The dentist checks your child's teeth and gums. They look for cavities, gum issues, or other problems. X-ray may be taken if needed for a better look.

Diagnosis & Treatment Plan

The dentist explains your child's dental health. If treatment is needed (like filling cavities or braces), they will explain the process. Parents and kids can ask questions and choose the best option.

Treatment Care

Teeth Cleaning:Removes plaque and keeps teeth healthy.

Fluoride Treatment:Protects teeth and prevents cavities.

Cavity Filling:Fixes damaged teeth using safe materials.

Braces & Aligners:Straightens teeth for a confident smile.

Follow-up & Preventive Care

To keep teeth healthy and prevent problems.

To fix small issues before they get worse.

To build a good habit of taking care of teeth.
