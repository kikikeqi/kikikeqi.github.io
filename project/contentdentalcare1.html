Tips for brushing your teeth

Time

Brush for at least 3 minutes each time to effectively remove food residue and bacteria.

Frequency

Brush your teeth every morning and evening, and rinse after meals. If possible, brush after each meal.

Replace

Replace your toothbrush regularly, generally every 3-4 months.

Prevent

Using dental floss and mouthwash can further clean the mouth and prevent oral diseases.

Clean

Brush for at least 3 minutes each time to effectively remove food residue and bacteria.
